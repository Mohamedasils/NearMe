KFC

KFC (Kentucky Fried Chicken) as a global fast-food chain: International Fast-Food Chain: KFC is a renowned international fast-food chain known for its fried chicken. Menu Variety: The menu typically includes a variety of fried chicken options, such as original recipe, extra crispy, and grilled chicken. Sides like mashed potatoes, coleslaw, and biscuits are also popular. Global Presence: KFC has a widespread global presence, with numerous outlets in different countries, adapting its menu to local tastes and preferences. Colonel Sanders: The brand is often associated with its iconic founder, Colonel Harland Sanders, whose image is used in the company's branding. Franchise Model: KFC operates on a franchise model, with many of its outlets being owned and operated by independent franchisees.
